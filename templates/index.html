Research by the Federal Bureau of Prisons shows that both male and female prison pen-pals who establish positive contacts, pursue

educational opportunities, and seek normalization through friendship, etc. are less likely to re-offend and maintain outside of prison walls.

Thats why we created pen-pals but instead of knowing exactly who your corresponding with theres a twist. You first enter your initail message

and we write your letter and send it to a random prisoner free of charge, we then process their response and mail it to you!
